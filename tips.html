Start with one tool

Don't enable everything at once. Master one tool before adding the next.

Be specific in your prompts

"Build a website" is not a spec. What's the purpose? Who's the audience?

Correct concisely

Don't let the agent go down the wrong path. Course-correct early.

End-of-session backups

Make it a habit: after every sillage, run a backup. Future you will thank you.
