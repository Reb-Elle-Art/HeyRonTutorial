How to Work Together_

The best partnerships have rhythm. Here's how to find yours.

Commands vs. Conversations

  • Commands: Short, routine tasks. "Check my emails." "Publish this."
  • Conversations: New territory. "Here's what I'm trying to do..."

Feedback Loops

Correct concisely. Don't apologize for corrections — just state what changed:

"Actually, I prefer X approach. Let's do it that way."

Initiative

Decide: Does the agent propose unprompted, or only act when asked? There's no right answer — but be consistent.
